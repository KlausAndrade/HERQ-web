What Happens After You Request Deletion

  1. Immediate: Your account will be marked for deletion
  2. Within 24 hours: We'll send a confirmation email
  3. Within 7 days: All your data will be permanently deleted
  4. Confirmation: You'll receive a final confirmation email

Data Retention Policy

We follow strict data retention policies:

  • Account data is deleted within 7 days of request
  • Backup data is purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for app improvement
  • Legal compliance data may be retained as required by law

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Right to be forgotten: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Right to object: Opt out of certain data processing
