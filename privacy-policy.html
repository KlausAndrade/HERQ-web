Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Quick Summary: HERQ stores your fitness data locally on your device. We don't sell or share your personal data. You have full control over your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account information (email, name) when you sign up
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Authentication data for login services (Facebook, Apple)

Fitness Data

  • Workout sessions and history
  • Exercise performance data (sets, reps, weights)
  • Personal records and achievements
  • Workout templates and preferences
  • Progress tracking data

Technical Data

  • App usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Device information for compatibility
  • Error logs for app improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To provide fitness tracking and workout management
  • Personalization: To customize your workout experience and recommendations
  • Progress Tracking: To show your fitness journey and achievements
  • App Improvement: To enhance features and fix issues
  • Communication: To send important updates and notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your fitness data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. This ensures your data remains private and accessible even offline.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored securely in our cloud infrastructure. You can disable this feature at any time.

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal fitness data with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing

5. Third-Party Services

HERQ integrates with these services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: Backend services and authentication
  • Facebook: Social login (optional)
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple (optional)
  • Analytics: App usage insights (anonymized)

6. Your Rights and Choices

Data Access

  • View all your stored data through the app
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Request a copy of your data

Data Control

  • Edit or update your information anytime
  • Delete specific workouts or data points
  • Disable cloud sync to keep data local only

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

HERQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you're using HERQ from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of HERQ after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@herq.app
  • Website: https://herq.app
  • Data Deletion: https://herq.app/delete-data
